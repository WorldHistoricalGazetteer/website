Welcome to this "alpha" release of Version 3 of the World Historical Gazetteer! Feedback on this work-in-progress is much appreciated.

WHG is a collaborative open platform for gathering information about historical place, providing a collection of content and services that permit world historians, their students, and the general public to do spatial and temporal reasoning and visualization in a data rich environment at global and trans-regional scales.
