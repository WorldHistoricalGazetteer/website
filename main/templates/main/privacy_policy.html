Privacy

You may use WHG freely without registering. If you choose to register, we require a name and a valid email address, which we will never share without your explicit permission. We may occasionally notify registered users of changes to the system or impending downtime that could affect their use of it.

Use of Turnstile

To protect against automated abuse (bots), WHG uses Cloudflare Turnstile. When Turnstile is displayed (for example, on forms submitted by unregistered users), your browser may communicate with Cloudflare’s servers to verify that you are a human visitor. This may involve the transmission of limited technical information (such as IP address and browser characteristics). Cloudflare does not use this information for advertising or cross-site tracking; it is used solely to ensure the security and integrity of WHG services.

ORCID Registration and Sign-In

If you choose to register or sign in using your ORCID iD, WHG will — with your permission — access your public ORCID record (name, iD, and affiliation) and any information visible to “Trusted parties”, including at least one verified email address. This information is stored securely in your WHG profile and used only for account management and necessary communication. After your account is created, we will ask separately for your consent to receive optional newsletters. You may revoke WHG’s access at any time through your ORCID account settings.

If you have any questions or concerns, please let us know via our Contact Form.
