Formatting Guidelines:
  • Separate multiple creators with a semicolon (;).
    Example: Karl Grossner; Stephen James Gadd
  • You may (optionally) add an ORCiD (Open Researcher and Contributor ID) after a personal name, which will be included in the DOI and citation metadata.
    Example: Karl Grossner (0000-0002-4066-8297)
  • Enclose organisation names in [square brackets] to indicate they are entities rather than individuals.
    Example: [University of Pittsburgh]
Full Example:
Ruth Mostern; Karl Grossner (0000-0002-4066-8297); Stephen James Gadd; [University of Pittsburgh]
