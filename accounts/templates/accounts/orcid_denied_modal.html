Why Connect?

Connecting your ORCiD iD with World Historical Gazetteer will allow:

  • WHG to maintain connections to your scholarly accomplishments.
  • You to receive proper attribution for your contributions to the WHG platform.

See other ways to make your ORCiD iD work for you.

Privacy and Security
  • WHG will not alter your ORCiD record in any way.
  • You may set to "private" any part of your ORCiD record, except your name and ORCiD iD number.
  • However, you will not be able to sign in to WHG if you do not authorize WHG as a Trusted party, and do not allow Trusted parties to see your email address.
  • WHG will never have access to your ORCiD password.
  • You may revoke your authorization at any time at https://orcid.org/account.
  • You are always in control of your ORCiD record. Learn more.
